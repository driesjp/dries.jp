WinControl 0.1 beta

Automation tool for Windows, written in C.
More of a proof of concept than anything at this point, but still a useful little tool for automation.

Click here to download

Functions

  • ATTACHPROCESS "processname" - Attach to a running process
  • BRINGTOFRONT - Bring window (from process) to front
  • CLICK x y - Click at specified coordinates
  • RIGHTCLICK x y - Right-click at specified coordinates
  • DOUBLECLICK x y - Double-click at specified coordinates
  • SENDKEYS "text" - Send keystrokes
  • SLEEP milliseconds - Wait for a specified time

Usage

WinControl.exe -s <script_file>

Example Script

ATTACHPROCESS "notepad.exe"
BRINGTOFRONT
SENDKEYS "Hello, world!"
CLICK 200 300
SLEEP 1000
RIGHTCLICK 400 500

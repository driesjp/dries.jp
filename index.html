Applications - Guitar notes mapper
Guitar notes mapper

 

Guitar notes mapper is an application to help visualise the notes of chords on a guitar neck.

Perhaps this may inspire someone to experiment instead of playing chord shapes, if they weren't already.

 

It can also be used to simply display some or all notes on the neck.

 

Windows 10+ (but should be 7+, though untested)
